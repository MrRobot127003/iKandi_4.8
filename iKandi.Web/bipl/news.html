Our New Head Office



I KANDI / BOUTIQUE are happy to announce that we have moved to our brand new head office in Hosiery Complex, Noida. This site will house an additional 300 stitching machines on the ground floor, giving us an increased production capacity of 250,000 garments per month. We will also have 50 sampling machines based in this office to help us with our increasing sampling requests.



3D-Plans for the new Head Office of Boutique international pvt Ltd. and Ikandi Fashion and Design Ltd.


3D Layout for Head Office

As a part of our business growth and to provide a larger manufacturing capacity, the company has recently bought 6,000 sq meters of land in the prime industrial estate in Noida, India. The total built up area will be 120,000 sq ft housing over 1800 machines. This will give an additional capacity of over 500,000 fashion garments to our clientele.
